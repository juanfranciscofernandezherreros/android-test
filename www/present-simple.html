1. Form

SymbolStructureExample
+verb / verb + -sShe works in London.
–do / does not + verbHe doesn’t work in London.
?do / does + verb?Where do you work?

2. Uses

  • To say when things happen regularly:
    That car always stops at two o’clock.
  • To talk about permanent situations:
    I work in London.
  • To state general truths:
    The moon goes round the earth.
  • To talk about habits:
    You buy new clothes every Saturday.
  • To describe book or film plots:
    The story begins and ends in Spain.
