Unit 1 – Present Simple

Teoría y ejemplos

Uso del Present Simple

Usamos el Present Simple para hablar de hábitos, rutinas y hechos generales.

  • I play football every Sunday.
  • She works in a hospital.
  • The sun rises in the east.

Forma

Affirmative: Subject + base verb (+s/es for he/she/it)

Negative: Subject + don’t/doesn’t + base verb

Question: Do/Does + subject + base verb?
